In memory to West

The rightest one was the bassist of Hämatom

The bassist of Hämatom, a band from Germany, was a great bass player. He died after a shortly heavy sickness. The band didn't told more about the sickness. It was totaly silent around the band, one month along. They have a new bass player, now. But it wil be never the same like before. They was friends since the school. R.I.P West.
